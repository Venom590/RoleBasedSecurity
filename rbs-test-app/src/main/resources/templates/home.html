Users

Users in this app are the users just as you know it. They have user names, passwords and names. They can be associated with roles. Roles can have certain rights (read, write, delete) on objects. Further on you can sign in by entering your credentials on the corresponding page.

View details »

Objects

Objects are simple abstract entities, which you can create, show, edit and delete. These are simple database entries with associated roles and the proper rights. So if you are in a role which has the proper rights (read, write, delete), you can perform certain actions.
